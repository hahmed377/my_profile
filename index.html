Autonomous Initio car

A project which i did during university was that i had to write code for an initio car so that it can at Autonomously. I had to program the car so it would look around using its camera to try and find a red blob. I also had to make sure that the also used it other sensors to be more aware of it surroundings. By using real time assurance it would mean that the initio car would behave like a real car as if it was driving and a obstacle comes in the way the car would come to an immediate halt. The real time behaviour i had to priorities was the speed of the car. I had to make sure that the car wasnt moving too slow yet moving too fast. I overcame this problem by putting an average speed that the initio car would move at and also putting delays in every second to make sure that the car had enough time to evaluate its environment and then move off again. I decided to put delays as it would be better as the car is very slow at processing the code and i had to make sure that the car wouldnt keep on driving.
